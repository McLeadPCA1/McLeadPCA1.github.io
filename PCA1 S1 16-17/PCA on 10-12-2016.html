Essential Question: How do we enchance a presentation with pictures, shapes, and wordArt using Microsoft PowerPoint?

Objective: Students will be able enchance a presentation with pictures, shapes, and wordArt using Microsoft PowerPoint.

Bellringer:

  • Please retrieve one of the Microsoft Office 2010 textbooks.
    The large textbook with the spiral binding.

  • Today you will finish your work on the In The Lab Assignments at the end of the chapter:

    • PPT130 - Fairytale Trip to Germany

    • PPT132 - Small Busines Loans

    • PPT134 - ABC's of DO-RE-ME

  • Today you will finish your work on the In The Lab Assignments at the end of the chapter:

    • PPT130 - Fairytale Trip to Germany

    • PPT132 - Small Busines Loans

    • PPT134 - ABC's of DO-RE-ME

  • After you have finished all of the In the Lab exercises you will create a PowerPoint on something that you are interested in. You will pick the topic, which can be anything so long as the topic is school appropriate. You may use Google Images for the pictures for the PowerPoint. Make sure you are done the assignments in the textbook BEFORE you begin your personal interest PowerPoint.

    However, your PowerPoint must contain the following criteria:

    • Six slides total for the PowerPoint presentation

    • Common theme applied throughout the presentation

    • A picture for every slide in the presentation

    • At least two slide backgrounds inserted

    • WordArt on at least two slides

    • Transitions for every slide
